01
Act One · Phases 1–3

The Infinity Saga

Clean. Grounded. Linear. A billionaire builds a suit. Universes haven't collided yet — they don't even know the others exist. Six Infinity Stones scattered across the cosmos. Six heroes learning to trust each other. One inevitable war.

Notice the UI: metallic blue and gold. Clean heroic lines. This is MCU Prime before the cracks.

Phase 1 — The Assembly
2008–2012 · 6 Films
Iron Man 2008
"A man in a cave builds his way out — and builds the Marvel Universe."
EssentialFilm
Tony Stark builds the first Iron Man suit in captivity. Where the MCU was born. Meet the sarcasm, the genius, and the beginning of everything. His arc from selfish billionaire to self-sacrificing hero is one of cinema's greatest.
➔ The origin of the entire MCU. Tony's journey across 20+ films starts here. "I am Iron Man" will mean something very different by Endgame.
Post-credits: Nick Fury appears. The Avengers Initiative is born. Everything starts now.

"A scientist who becomes a monster, and can't stop running."
OptionalFilm
Bruce Banner on the run from the military, searching for a cure. Edward Norton plays Banner; he's later recast as Mark Ruffalo.
➔ Abomination reappears in She-Hulk. Otherwise largely standalone — safe to skip, fine to watch.
Iron Man 2 2010
"Tony is dying, but he won't slow down."
RecommendedFilm
Tony faces a dying arc reactor and a vengeful Russian villain. Introduces Black Widow properly, expands S.H.I.E.L.D.'s role.
➔ Introduces Black Widow and deepens the SHIELD/Fury relationship. More connective tissue than essential viewing.
Thor 2011
"A god must become worthy of his power — and fall in love with Earth."
EssentialFilmCosmic
A god is stripped of his power and cast to Earth. Introduces Asgard, Loki, and the cosmic mythology of the MCU. The Norse mythology becomes real.
➔ Loki becomes the MCU's most complex, beloved villain. His arc across six appearances begins here. This is where the multiverse story truly starts.
Captain America: The First Avenger 2011
"A small man with a big heart — frozen in ice, waiting for a world that needed him."
EssentialFilm
A frail young man from Brooklyn becomes a super soldier in WWII. Introduces the Tesseract — the very first Infinity Stone. Steve Rogers' origin and the sacrifice that defines him.
➔ The Tesseract / Space Stone drives the entire Infinity Saga. Cap's moral core is the ethical backbone against which Tony's arc is measured.
The Avengers 2012
"The moment every separate thread became one story."
EssentialFilmCosmic
Loki arrives with the Mind Stone and an alien army. Fury assembles every hero we've met. "New York" becomes the MCU's founding myth. Thanos appears in a post-credits scene — four years before his actual arrival.
➔ The crossover event that proved shared universes could work. The moment "I am Iron Man" stops being a selfish declaration and becomes a promise.
Phase 2 — The Expansion
2013–2015 · 6 Films
Iron Man 32013
"Tony Stark stripped of his armour — and forced to be a man."
RecommendedFilm
Tony has PTSD from the Battle of New York. His identity, his suits, and his relationships are all stripped away. He discovers what's underneath when everything is gone.
➔ Completes the Tony Stark character arc begun in Iron Man 1. Important for understanding Tony in Endgame.
Captain America: The Winter Soldier2014
"The most grounded Marvel thriller. SHIELD falls. Trust becomes the real weapon."
EssentialFilm
Steve Rogers discovers SHIELD has been infiltrated by HYDRA from within. The paranoia, the betrayal, the action — this film plays like a 70s political thriller with superheroes. HYDRA's fall reshapes the MCU's infrastructure permanently.
➔ SHIELD's destruction fundamentally changes the MCU power structure. Sets up Civil War's political landscape.
Guardians of the Galaxy2014
"The MCU's cosmic door opens — and out comes a talking raccoon and a dancing twig."
EssentialFilmCosmic
A team of misfits and criminals fights over the Power Stone. The MCU suddenly has space, alien races, and emotional comedy-drama at a cosmic scale. Introduces the Power Stone and the galactic context for Thanos.
➔ First major use of the Power Stone. Thanos's cosmic ambitions become concrete. The Guardians converge with the Avengers in Infinity War.
Captain America: Civil War2016
"The Avengers break each other — and the MCU is never the same."
EssentialFilm
Tony and Steve fight over government oversight, then over something far more personal. The team fractures. Introduces Black Panther and the MCU's Spider-Man. By the end, the Avengers are broken — just in time for Thanos.
➔ The division between Tony and Steve makes the Infinity War defeat feel inevitable. This fracture echoes through Endgame. Peter Parker joins the MCU here.
Phase 3 — The Reckoning

Thor: Ragnarok2017
"Asgard is not a place. It's a people. And the ship is sailing toward Thanos."
EssentialFilmCosmic

➔ Tony and Peter's relationship. Peter's arc from Homecoming to Endgame to No Way Home is one of the MCU's finest character journeys.

➔ "Wakanda forever" is both a promise and a location. The Battle of Wakanda in Infinity War hits harder knowing this world deeply.

Act Two · Phase 4 · 2021–2022

The Multiverse Opens

The clean linear MCU ends here. Timelines fracture. Variants of your heroes appear. The TVA emerges from beyond time. Wanda goes mad with grief. Reality is no longer reliable.

Notice the UI shifting — the glow is violet now. The lines are less clean. Something is wrong with time.

TIME VARIANCE AUTHORITY — CLASSIFICATION: ACTIVE
An organisation outside of time itself. They prune "variants" — people who deviate from the Sacred Timeline. Everything you watched in Act 1 is just one of infinite possible timelines. The TVA was suppressing all the others.

Understanding the TVA is essential for No Way Home, Multiverse of Madness, Quantumania, Deadpool & Wolverine, and Doomsday. Every multiverse event traces back here.
🌀
Variants
"A different version of you, in a different timeline, who made a different choice.
They are not you. But they are also entirely you."
VARIANTS: When a person deviates from the Sacred Timeline — takes a different path at a key moment — the TVA classifies them as a Variant. Nexus Events create Variants. Loki, He Who Remains, and eventually every Spider-Man and X-Man we know are Variants of each other.
WandaVision2021
"Grief is love with nowhere to go — so she made an entire reality for it."
EssentialSeriesMultiverse
Wanda traps a New Jersey town in a sitcom reality to cope with losing Vision. Every episode format — 1950s, 70s, 90s, 2000s — mirrors her deteriorating mental state. When reality cracks, so does she. The Scarlet Witch is born.
➔ Directly and essentially sets up Doctor Strange: Multiverse of Madness. Wanda's transformation is completely broken without this context. The Darkhold is introduced here.
Loki — Season 12021
"The god of mischief discovers the most terrifying thing of all: he's not unique."
EssentialSeriesTVAMultiverse
The escaped Loki from Endgame is captured by the TVA. He discovers there are infinite variants of himself. At the end of the Sacred Timeline lives He Who Remains — the man holding the multiverse together. He is killed. Everything branches.
➔ The single most important show for understanding the entire Multiverse Saga. Without this, No Way Home, MoM, Quantumania, Deadpool & Wolverine, and Doomsday all lose half their meaning.
He Who Remains: His death triggers the multiverse collapse — the root cause of everything in Acts 3 and 4.
The Falcon and the Winter Soldier2021
"What does it mean for a Black man to carry the Captain America shield?"
RecommendedSeries
Sam Wilson struggles to take up the shield. A grounded, politically sharp story about race, legacy, and what the Captain America identity means in the real world. Sam becomes Captain America by earning it.
➔ Sets up Sam's role as Captain America going forward. Important for the larger post-Endgame world.
Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness2022
"Every choice has a version of you that made the wrong one. And she's already watching."
EssentialFilmMultiverse
Strange tumbles through multiple universes alongside America Chavez. Wanda, corrupted by the Darkhold, hunts her own variants' children. Sam Raimi's horror sensibility transforms the MCU's visual language. Incursions are introduced — and they're how universes die.
➔ INCURSIONS are the mechanical basis of Doomsday. Universes colliding and destroying each other. This is not metaphor — it's the central threat of Secret Wars.
💥
Incursions
"A collision between universes.
If left unchecked… both realities die."
INCURSIONS: When two universes collide, one destroys the other — or both are annihilated simultaneously. Every time a person travels between timelines or alters a sacred event, the risk of an Incursion increases. In Secret Wars, Doctor Doom harnesses the last Incursion to become God. This is the mechanic of Doomsday.

Collision of Universes

Fox's X-Men. Three Spider-Men from three eras. Legacy Marvel characters who never touched the MCU — are now touching. Everything connects. Universes you thought were separate histories are now the same story.

Universe Branches

Before No Way Home, explore these separate universes. Each one has its own identity, its own history. They're about to collide with yours.

Earth-10005
Fox X-Men Universe
Darker blue + amber. Gritty, operatic, traumatized. The mutants never got their happy ending — until Deadpool & Wolverine gave them a memorial.
X-Men: First Class Days of Future Past Logan Deadpool 1 & 2
▶ Essential Fox path
The essential three: X-Men: First Class → Days of Future Past → Logan.

Logan (2017) is a masterpiece of superhero cinema — a grief-soaked Western about mortality. Watch it before Deadpool & Wolverine. The entire weight of D&W depends on you loving that Wolverine.

Days of Future Past directly sets up the timeline mechanics that the TVA later exploits.

Raimi Spider-Man
Red + deep green. Nostalgic, operatic, gothic. Sam Raimi made Spider-Man 2 in 2004 — still one of the greatest superhero films ever made.

▶ The Tobey path
Essential: Spider-Man 1 & 2. Spider-Man 2 is arguably the greatest Spider-Man film until Into the Spider-Verse. Alfred Molina's Doctor Octopus returns in No Way Home — watch his origin first.

Spider-Man 3 is messier but contains the emotional wound (Gwen-parallel) that Tobey's arc in No Way Home quietly heals.
Earth-120703
Amazing Spider-Man
Electric blue. Sharp, modern, heartbreaking. Andrew Garfield's arc is defined by a loss he cannot fix — until No Way Home.

Watch Amazing Spider-Man 2 before No Way Home. Gwen Stacy's death is the wound that Andrew's entire arc in No Way Home is written around. His moment of redemption in NWH cannot land the way it does without this context.

That mid-air catch is one of the most emotionally earned moments in the entire MCU.

Act Four · Phase 6 · The End

Road to Doomsday

Victor von Doom. The incursions are real. Every thread — MCU, Fox, Spider-Verse, street level — is now converging. The branches are colliding. Universes are dying. This is what every story has been building toward.

🌍💥🌍
The Multiversal War
"Infinite universes. All of them dying.
One man willing to do what no hero will — hold what remains together by force."
THE MULTIVERSAL WAR: With He Who Remains dead and the Sacred Timeline splintered, infinite universes are colliding via Incursions. Doctor Doom — from the Fantastic Four's universe — has studied the Beyonder science behind all this. In Secret Wars comics, Doom harnesses the final Incursion to become God Emperor. This is where the MCU is heading.
The Fantastic Four: First Steps2025
"Marvel's First Family arrives from the past — and with them, the man who will break everything."
EssentialFilmMultiverse
Marvel's First Family arrives from a 1960s-aesthetic alternate universe. Reed Richards, Sue Storm, Johnny Storm, Ben Grimm face Galactus. Their universe is already incursion territory. And from their world also comes Victor von Doom.
➔ Direct prequel to Doomsday. Doom and Reed's rivalry — science versus mastery, intelligence versus control — is the emotional core beneath the multiversal war.
Avengers: Doomsday2026
"Every universe. Every hero. One war. Doctor Doom presides over everything."
EssentialFilmMultiverse
Doctor Doom steps into the role of multiversal architect. Incursions are destroying universes. Every hero from every universe you have watched is colliding here. The Multiverse Saga reaches its first, catastrophic peak.
➔ This is what the entire journey has been building toward. You are ready.
Avengers: Secret Wars2027
"Every universe fighting for the right to exist. One will survive. Maybe."
EssentialFilmMultiverse
The final chapter. Every surviving hero from every timeline. MCU, Fox, Spider-Verse, and beyond — all fighting for existence. In the comics, only one universe survives. The MCU rebuilds from what remains.
➔ The conclusion of the Multiverse Saga. The comics suggest Doctor Doom becomes God Emperor. The MCU inherits this mythology and makes it its own.
